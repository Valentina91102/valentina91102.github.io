Summer of Science

Introduction

This summer I had the pleasure of doing a National Science Foundation (NSF) Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU) with Mote Marine Laboratory in Sarasota, FLorida. I was selected to work with the Phytoplankton Ecology Lab, and undergo a study that would contribute to an overall project the lab had. My research focused on the mitigation technqies used against Microcystis, a phytoplankton considered a harmful algal bloom (HAB).
